About Daisy:

Daisy's Jewelers was born from love of faith, family, and handcrafted goods. Our founder carefully designs each rosary and other jewelery piece to be a meaningful reminder of prayer and protection, and the beauty in everyday life.

Each design is made by hand and each color is thoughtfully picked so that each color compliments each other and is very beautifully crafted. Our Rosaries are the perfect gift for a birthday, sacrament, or as a personal keepsake.
